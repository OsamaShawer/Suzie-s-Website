10 Facts learned from "When Zakary Came To Town"

1- Hard challenges show the truth real friendship : When: Toby and Cal stick by each other after Wayne dies, even when they both feel lost and angry.

2- Dreams to be achieved needs some sacrifices : When: Toby's mother traveled to Nashville, to achieve his dream to song, but that made Toby feeling bad and depressed because there is no anyone to be kind with him.

3- Repulations are not the determiners who the person is : When: Zakary is called 'The fatest person' but he was so kind and patient ( Better than other people ).

4- Every person should have people he loves them and belongs to : When: Zackary was alone in the town at first, but he became friend with Toby and Cal ( He finds himself loving Toby and Cal ).

5- Challenges teaches the person to be stronger : When: Toby faced a lot of challenges like Family changes, and the bad fear feelings, but he was helping Zakary and works with resturants, and mowing grass ( he was strong ).

6- Favours and being kind with the others make a beautiful feeling, great repulation about you, and greate legacy : When: Toby and Cal helped Zakary by getting him food and talk with him ( sit with him ), which built trust between them and the community.

7- Every person has hidden feelings or ideas that is hard to be shown: > When: Toby has bad feelings all of the story, but no one noticed that from his friends.

8- Family can be chosen, not your brothers or sisters only : When: Toby, Cal and Zakary found themselves helping each other whatever the thing is.

9- The appearance of the body of a person doesn't matter ( Because its Allah creation ) the important thing is the behavior : When: Toby and Cal became friends with Zakary but he is fat and ugly.

10- The beautiful memories is essential to get stronger : When: Toby thinks and remembers the beautiful moments with his mother, makes him stronger to do the impossible.
